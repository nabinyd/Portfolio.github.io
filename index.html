My Works

Uddharan

I developed a donation app called "Uddharan" using Flutter. Uddharan is designed to facilitate donations and connect donors with those in need. The app features a user-friendly interface, making it easy for users to browse donation opportunities, track their contributions, and stay updated on the impact of their donations.

BMI App

I developed a BMI app using Flutter to help users easily calculate and track their Body Mass Index. The app features a simple and intuitive interface where users can input their height and weight to instantly get their BMI results. By utilizing Flutter, I ensured the app runs smoothly on both Android and iOS platforms. This project demonstrates my ability to create user-centric and health-focused mobile applications.

Health monitoring system using IoT

During a hackathon, I built a health monitoring system using a pulse rate sensor and temperature sensor. This system allows people to check their pulse rate and body temperature, and the data can be accessed from anywhere on the internet. It's a helpful tool for keeping track of one's health remotely.

Hydroponics system using Machine learning
